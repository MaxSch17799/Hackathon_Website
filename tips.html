Workflow (click to expand)
1) Ideation ▶
Ask AI to challenge the idea and ask clarifying questions. Let it poke holes early.
2) Planning ▶
Write a quick planning doc, have a second agent review it, then split into chunks for parallel agents.
3) Execution ▶
Don’t hand-write everything. Run multiple agents, keep an agents.md/readme.md, ask for docs/comments, and use screenshots or PowerPoint mockups as inputs.
4) Review / Debug ▶
Paste logs, switch agents when stuck, and add screenshots to help the AI see what’s wrong.
